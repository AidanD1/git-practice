Hi, my name is Rachelle and I have some advice for you!

Small details matter...A LOT!

Pay attention to the details, especially when your code is not working like you think it should. When it comes to styling your HTML, if nothing you are doing in CSS is changing anything on your page, your CSS and HTML selector names probably don't match. For instance, in your HTML, one of your selectors may be skill list. Then in your CSS, you may have accidentally made the selector skills list. Small errors in your selector names will prevent your HTML and CSS from communicating and your wonderful CSS code will not work.

Don't be afraid to ask questions...I should have!

The previous advice was hard learned by me. It took me a long time to figure it out. I became very frustrated and discouraged. I should have asked for help. Don't be like me. If you have checked everything, verified your code, and run through all your steps and something is still not working, ask!
